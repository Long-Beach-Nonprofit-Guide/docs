NationBuilder is a software platform that is designed to help organizations manage their online presence, engage with supporters, and raise funds. It offers a variety of tools and features that can help your organization build a website, create email campaigns, and track donor contributions, effectively filling the role of a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform and Content Manangement System (CMS) for small organizations. The platform is easy to use and can help your organization reach its goals more effectively.
